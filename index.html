Andrea Boskovic

I'm graduating with a masters degree from the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington in December 2024, advised by Jon Wakefield. My research here was generously supported by the DoD NDSEG Fellowship. I've worked on survey methodology and spatio-temporal modeling of mortality in low- and middle-income countries, and I've previously studied optimal resource allocation and methods for imbalanced data classification during undergrad at Amherst College.
