Andrea Boskovic

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington, advised by Jon Wakefield. My research here is generously supported by the DoD NDSEG Fellowship.

I'm currently working on survey methodology and spatio-temporal modeling of mortality in low- and middle-income countries. I previously studied optimal resource allocation and methods for imbalanced data classification during undergrad at Amherst College.
