About Me

I'm a senior at Amherst College studying Math & Statistics with interests in data science and machine learning. I work in the Carter Lab doing statistical modeling, and I'm writing a thesis on neural network methods for imbalanced image data classification. In my free time, I love running, playing violin, and listening to music. Some of my favorite artists are The Beatles, Amy Winehouse, and Bon Iver, and this short playlist includes a few of my favorite songs.

Originally, I'm from Bosnia and Herzegovina, but I've spent most of my life in Connecticut. I spend lots of time with my cat, Frki, who is a great coding buddy!
